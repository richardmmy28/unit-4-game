Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are three Starcraft icons below. By clicking on an icon you will add a specific

amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose

the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each ico is hidden from you until you click on it.
